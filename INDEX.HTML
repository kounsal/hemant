ABOUT HEMANT ADWANI

Hemant Adwani is a young and bright singer from the capital city of India, New Delhi. Started off uploading videos of his singing on social media platforms, he has now become a recognised and appreciated performer. He could never imagined to be as popular as he has become today. Hemant has been imparted formal training in classical singing. Whenever he used to get time, he would practice for hours at a stretch. Hemant has upped his skills by learning to play guitar. Singing and playing guitar is a favourite for every artist. He has performed with many bands and solo performers as well. His dual skills in singing and playing guitar, makes him a favourite pick for stage shows and small concerts. Hemant loves to balance both the roles and practices on a daily basis, sometimes with other bands and his friends. Inching closer to chasing his dream of becoming a singer, he has started performing at many events such as campus gigs, private functions, and also playing at city’s popular pubs and restaurants. Hemant's melodious guitar strumming over his soulful voice makes the audience sway their hands and become mesmerized. This is something which sets him really apart from other singers.

Online And Offline Music Classes

Looking into the need of the hour we conduct both online and offline music classes. The teaching learning pedagogy that we follow in our online and offline music classes is entirely different. Our online singing classes cater to Indian students . Whether you are taking guitar classes online or offline we make sure that the intricacies are not missed and we give you a strong foundation. To ensure learning during online music classes we support the students with appropriate teaching learning material, videos and content that helps them to assimilate the knowledge. We keep the number of students in offline classes very limited so every student is taken care of and customised learning can take place.
